About

I am a PhD scholar in International Development Studies at Utrecht University and the Institute for Social and Economic Change, Bengaluru. My research focuses on population studies, ageing, health, inequality, and the political economy of development. I combine quantitative and qualitative approaches to address policy-relevant questions in India and the Global South.
